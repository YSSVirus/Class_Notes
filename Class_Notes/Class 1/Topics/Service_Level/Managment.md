The purpose of SLM is to ensure that all  
current and planned services are delivered to  
targets through a constant cycle of  
negotiating, agreeing, monitoring and  
reviewing  
Defining, documenting, agreeing, monitoring,  
measuring, reporting and reviewing the quality level of  
IT services  
Instigating corrective measures when needed  
Improving the communication between the business  
and customer  
Ensuring that specific targets are developed for all  
services  
Monitoring customer satisfaction with the quality of  
the service  
Ensuring that the customer has a clear expectation of  
quality level that will be delivered


Service Level Management (SLM) is a  
vital process that is responsible for the  
agreement and documentation of service  
level targets and responsibilities within SLAs  
and service level requirements (SLRs) for  
each service.
